# Prototype Tactical Guides

Spincore currently renders subtle on-field target highlights so tactics can be
evaluated during prototype play. These guides visualize live tactical jobs and
are deliberately separate from movement, physics, and possession rules.

They are a testing aid, not the final strategy interface. A future game-facing
presentation may use:

- offense cards with player positions and passing lanes
- defense cards with zones or marking assignments
- a pre-match formation and strategy screen

That card and diagram UI is intentionally outside the current prototype scope.
